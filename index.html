Communications

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored using secure infrastructure controlled by the Company. Access to databases is strictly limited to authorized personnel. Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are applied to protect user data.

Message Privacy and Exceptional Access

The Company does not routinely monitor or read private messages. Access may occur only in exceptional circumstances, including user requests, legal obligations, safety concerns, or dispute resolution. Such access is limited, logged, and performed solely for compliance or protection.

Data Retention and Deletion

Users may delete posts, comments, images, and messages. Upon deletion, content is permanently removed from active databases and is no longer accessible.

The Company does not share personal data with third parties, advertisers, or external organizations. Data remains within Company-controlled systems.

Public Profiles

User accounts are public by default. Content shared publicly may be viewed by other users.
